HOW DOES IT WORK?

Pilots of the sport of paragliding can record their entire flight using a simple device. This recording will be saved as an IGC file, which you can simply insert into the system via our website and we will generate an interactive map on which you will see the entire flight progress in 2D and 3D view.




What weoffer

2D view

The possibility to view your flight record in 2D form on our interactive map. All you need is an IGC file of the flight to upload to the system.

3D view

You can also view the footage in 3D on an interactive map. You will also only need the IGC file from your flight to upload to the system.

Animation

We also offer the option to view animation of flight on an interactive map. You only need to provide the IGC file from your flight to play animation.

Save Map

You can easily save your map from flight as .png file. Just import your flight and click on Save Map button.
